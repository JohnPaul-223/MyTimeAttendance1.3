          4) Biometric Import

          Import a text-based .dat, .txt, or .csv export from your biometric device. Existing users are matched by email, email username, or employee name.

          If the export does not contain explicit IN / OUT labels, the importer alternates punches as check in then check out per employee per day.
          Imported biometric records use the saved geofence center as placeholder coordinates because biometric export files do not include GPS data.
